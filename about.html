AGORA - A public open space used for assemblies and markets.

-What is Agora?-

Agora is an attempt to create an expandable, multipurpose space in VRChat that can be many things to many people.
-Dance clubs
-Hangouts
-Storefronts
-Showrooms
-Screening venues
-Convention centers

Agora is a "mini-megaworld," situated in a formerly-abandoned mall and offering users the opportunity to fill the empty stores.

Inspired by VRChat's existing megaworlds, as well as events like Vket and Metafes.
